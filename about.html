About Us

UPP Global is a Bermudian led project, currently operating in Vietnam. UPP is an acronym for United Peoples Project and is short for the word 'uplifting'. The word uplifting is defined as "inspiring happiness, optimism, or hope". This definition is an accurate description of what UPP Global’s projects achieve.

We work towards uplifting and inspiring young people.

Our primary objective is to contribute to the reduction of youth inequality worldwide, through the advancement of sport and education.

In 2020, inequality was reported as growing for more than “70 percent of the global population, exacerbating the risks of divisions and hampering economic and social development” (United Nations).

We seek to provide new opportunities for all people, with a focus on disadvantaged youth and youth from vulnerable communities as a response to the growing threat of inequality worldwide. We aim to achieve this through various purposeful projects that are focused on providing equal opportunities for the young people that are involved.

UPP Global seeks to contribute to UN Sustainable Development

Goal Number 4: Education

and

Goal Number 10: Reduced Inequalities

Goal 4 aims to provide quality education for all youth worldwide. Goal 10 aims to reduce inequalities based on income, sex, age, disability, sexual orientation, race, class, ethnicity, religion, and opportunity.

Through our tailor-made projects, we will reduce inequality based on the criteria provided by the UNSDG.

We will achieve this mission by exposing young people to new sports and education-based opportunities.

We invite you to learn more about growing inequality, the effects of COVID-19, and ways you can influence change. If you believe in our mission of equality, please join us by donating now.
