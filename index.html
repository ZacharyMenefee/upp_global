UPP Global

We believe in a world where all people are equal, have access to equal oppourtunities, and are given the tools needed to thrive in this world.

Our Mission

To uplift the community by contributing to United Nations Sustainable Development Number 4: Quality Education and Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities.

Through the advancement of education and sport we empower youth, provide opportunities for young people and work towards a reduction in inequality worldwide.

What We Do

Our projects aim to provide all children and youth free access to quality education and other opportunities.

We work towards reducing inequalities based on income, sex, age, disability, sexual orientation, race, class, ethnicity, religion and opportunity.

We tackle world issues through the creation of tailor made projects that are designed to uplift and inspire those who are involved.

Facts:

  • In 2020 inequality grew for more than 70% of the global population.
  • 91% of students worldwide have been impacted by the temporary closures of schools.
  • In April 2020, close to 1.6 billion children and youth were out of school .
  • The effects of COVID-19 have exacerbated this issue, with young people and children the most vulnerable groups affected by growing inequality.
  • “One in five persons reported being discriminated against on at least one ground of discrimination prohibited by international human rights law”.

- UN Sustainable Development Goals
